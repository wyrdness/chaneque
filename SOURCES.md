> - Do not trivialize or appropriate these beliefs

## Respect Guidelines for Researchers

When engaging with Chaneque traditions:

1. **Consult Communities**: Always seek permission from indigenous communities
2. **Acknowledge Sovereignty**: Recognize indigenous intellectual property rights
3. **Cultural Context**: Present beliefs within proper cultural and spiritual context
4. **Avoid Appropriation**: Do not trivialize, commercialize, or misrepresent
5. **Give Credit**: Properly attribute indigenous knowledge to source communities
6. **Share Benefits**: If research benefits from indigenous knowledge, reciprocate appropriately
7. **Respect Privacy**: Some knowledge may be sacred or private; respect boundaries
